About the Event

The National Conference on Recent Advancements in Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence (NCRAMLAI 2025) will be held on 29th June 2025 at the Indian Statistical Institute, Hyderabad. This event brings together corporate professionals, academicians, and students to exchange knowledge and present research in the AI/ML domain.
